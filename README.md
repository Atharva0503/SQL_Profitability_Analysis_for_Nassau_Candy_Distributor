## 📌 Project Overview

This project delivers a **high-level business analytics solution** focused on **profitability optimization and cost efficiency** for a candy distribution company.

While traditional analysis focuses on revenue, this project uncovers **hidden inefficiencies behind revenue growth**, including:

* Margin leakage
* Cost-heavy operations
* Underperforming product lines
* Profit concentration risk

It demonstrates a **complete end-to-end analytics pipeline**, transforming raw transactional data into **strategic business insights** using:

* Excel → Data Cleaning & Validation
* SQL → Advanced Analytical Modeling
* Power BI → Executive Dashboard

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Identify **true profit drivers** across product lines
* Detect **high-revenue but low-margin products (profit illusion)**
* Quantify **cost inefficiencies and margin leakage**
* Apply **Pareto (80/20) analysis** to understand profit concentration
* Enable **data-driven pricing, product, and operational strategies**

## 📈 Key Insights

* Revenue alone is misleading — **profitability depends on margin**
* A small % of products generate majority of profit (Pareto effect)
* High sales products can still **destroy margins**
* Cost inefficiencies are the **primary driver of profit loss**
* Profitability varies significantly across regions
* Logistics decisions directly impact margins

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

* Prioritize **high-margin, high-profit products**

* Improve cost structure in inefficient divisions
* Standardize pricing strategies across regions
* Optimize logistics to reduce operational costs
